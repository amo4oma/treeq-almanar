Our Story

>

Welcome to Treeq AL-Manar, the leading choice for all your local transportation, customs clearance and supply chain services. Our mission is to make managing the chain of transport services, customs clearance and local supplies easy and free of effort and losses. We provide comprehensive solutions for all types of goods and services, including air, sea and land freight - by trucks, in addition to warehousing services, customs clearance services as well as general procurement services. Our network of experts and infrastructure enables you to access the best resources available in the local and foreign markets, as we provide you with a team of dedicated professionals, modern systems, transportation fleet and warehousing assets to assist you in all practical aspects relating to your business needs, including transportation, customs clearance, warehousing and supply.

transportation services

In Tareeq Al Manar Road, we offer a variety of transportation services tailored to meet all your needs. Logistics and customs clearance services

Preparation of customs documents: We prepare all documents and forms necessary for customs clearance, including documents for importers and exporters, declarations, proof of residence, and identity documents. We also provide assistance in completing any additional paperwork required by the customs authorities. • Communication with Customs Authorities: We communicate with appropriate customs authorities to ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations. We provide timely updates on the status of shipments and ensure all paperwork is submitted in a timely manner. • Customs Compliance: We ensure compliance with all customs laws, regulations and procedures by adhering to all relevant international, national and local legislation. We also provide advice and guidance on any changes in customs regulations and procedures.
